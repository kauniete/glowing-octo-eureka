Theme 03: Basic Content

Part one. 03.01.01 Tell a story without words

After a very short instruction, we had to create a video in images alone: no words could be spoken. Sound and music were allowed for atmospheric purposes, the allowed duration was up to 2 minutes and we were supposed to use our smartphones for filming. Out of three general themes, we chose "People who are too busy". For this video I came up with the story idea, a background song (from movie Nordkraft) and was the main "actress".
